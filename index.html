Frequently Asked Questions

What membership plans do you offer?

We offer flexible membership options to suit your needs:

  • Monthly Plan: Perfect for short-term commitments
  • Quarterly Plan: Save 15% compared to monthly rates
  • Annual Plan: Our best value with 25% savings

All plans include unlimited access to our facilities, free group classes, and exclusive member perks.

Can I try the gym before joining?

Absolutely! We offer two convenient ways to experience Oxygen:

  • Free 1-Day Trial Pass: Full access to facilities and classes
  • Guided Tour: Personal walkthrough with our staff

Book your trial through our website or visit us during staffed hours. No obligation or pressure to join.

What training support do you provide?

We offer comprehensive training support:

  • Personal Training: One-on-one sessions with certified trainers
  • Custom Programs: Tailored workout and nutrition plans
  • Group Classes: Various fitness styles and intensity levels
  • Form Check: Regular technique assessment sessions

Our trainers are certified professionals with years of experience in bodybuilding and fitness.

What amenities are included?

Your membership includes access to premium amenities:

  • State-of-the-art equipment and machines
  • Luxury locker rooms with showers
  • Sauna and recovery area
  • Protein shake bar
  • Free parking
  • 24/7 security monitored facility
How do I get started?

Getting started at Oxygen is easy:

  1. Book a free trial or tour online
  2. Meet with our team for a fitness assessment
  3. Choose your membership plan
  4. Get your personalized workout program

We'll guide you through every step to ensure you're comfortable and confident in beginning your fitness journey.
